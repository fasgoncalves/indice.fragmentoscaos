📘 Book Summary – Direct Democracy: From Stage to People

📅 2025-04-05

Direct Democracy: From Stage to People is a satirical and thought-provoking journey through the absurd theatre of modern representative politics — and a bold call to bring power back to where it belongs: the people. Francisco Gonçalves blends humour, civic poetry and biting critique to expose the cracks in traditional party systems, the emptiness of electoral promises, and the distant rituals of institutions that no longer listen.
