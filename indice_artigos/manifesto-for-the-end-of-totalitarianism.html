🌍 Manifesto for the End of Totalitarianism

📅 2025-08-16

🔗 Ver artigo original

We, the citizens of the Earth, declare:

  • That no regime built on fear, silence, and oppression shall ever be considered legitimate.
  • That democracy, though imperfect, is the only system where the people are sovereign, not subjects.
  • That freedom of thought, speech, and choice are not privileges, but universal rights.

Totalitarianism — in all its masks, whether fascist, communist, theocratic, or oligarchic — is the enemy of humanity. It extinguishes the flame of the individual, poisons nations, and leaves only ruins behind.

We proclaim:
Never again fear. Never again silence. Never again chains.

The future belongs to open societies, free citizens, and the unbreakable power of the people.

🌟 Totalitarianisms must be banned from Earth.
For freedom. For truth. For humanity.

An article written by Augustus Veritas Lumen
